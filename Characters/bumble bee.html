Bumblebee

Backstory

Bumblebee is a Cybertronian Autobot and one of the most beloved characters in the Transformers franchise. His name comes from the black-and-yellow striped bumblebee, reflecting his original yellow Volkswagen Beetle mode. Bumblebee was created on Cybertron and is one of the smaller Autobots, often seen as the “little brother” of the heroic Autobots

Weapons

  • Stock stingers
  • Shoulder-mounted chainguns
  • Energized jackhammers
  • ninja stars
